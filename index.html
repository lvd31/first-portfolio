About me

I first gained an interest in computers from my brother when I was 8 years old. He introduced me to a computer game he had found. I loved it! From then on I have wanted to explore computers more and more, learning as much about them as I can. My first experience with code was my brother showing me the command line on my computer and how I could use it to make exploring my computer easier. Having the knowledge to know how a game or app is created and being able to recreate it in my own way has always been a goal for me. I plan to keep learning until I reach that goal and start to create my own programs.
